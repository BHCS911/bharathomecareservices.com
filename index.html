    ABOUT US

    We at Bharat Home Care Services, provides a wide range of supportive and medical services to the individuals in the comfort their own homes. These services are typically by trained professionals, caregivers, or home health aids and are designed to assist with a range of needs including Activities of Daily Living ( ADLs), Medication Management, Medical Care, Companionship, Household Tasks, Respite Care and Specialised Care. Our goal of home care services is to enable individuals to live in their own homes as independently and comfortably as possible while receiving the care and assistance they need. Our services can benefit a wide range of people, including seniors,individuals with disabilities, those recovering from surgery or illnesses, and those with chronic health conditions. Our home care services can be a valuable alternative to institutionalised care in nursing home or hospitals.

    Our Services

    WE@ BHARAT HOME CARE SERVICES PROVIDES

    GERIATRIC CARE

    Geriatric care involves specialized health services designed to meet the unique needs of elderly individuals. It encompasses medical, social, and emotional support to enhance the well-being and quality of life for seniors, addressing age-related health conditions and promoting independence during the aging process.

    PATIENT CARE

    Patient care is the provision of medical, emotional, and practical support to individuals seeking health services. It involves addressing the patient's needs, ensuring their comfort, and promoting recovery through attentive and empathetic assistance from healthcare professionals. Patient care includes monitoring health conditions, administering treatments, and fostering a supportive environment to enhance overall well-being.

    PRE & POST DELIVERY CARE

    Pregnancy delivery care involves comprehensive support provided to expectant mothers before, during, and after childbirth. It includes emotional assistance, and practical guidance to ensure a healthy pregnancy and a safe delivery. This care encompasses aiming to promote the well-being of both the mother and the newborn child.

    BABY CARE

    Baby care involves providing nurturing and essential support to infants for their well-being and development. This includes feeding, diapering, ensuring a safe environment, and offering emotional comfort. Baby care also involves regular healthcare check-ups and fostering a loving and responsive relationship between caregivers and the baby, promoting a healthy and happy start to life..
